fun1=function(arg1,arg2){
  v=(1:100)
  m=c()
  for(i in v){
    if(i<5 | i>90){
      m[i]=v[i]*arg1
    }else{
      m[i]=v[i]*arg2

}

Additional

Prove the last foot note true.

Actually, people often use more for-loops than necessary. The ToDo above can be done more easily and quickly without a for-loop but with regular vector computations.

fun1=function(arg1,arg2){
  a=(1:4)
  b=(5:90)
  c=(91:100)
  m=c(a*arg1,b*arg2,c*arg1)
  m
}
